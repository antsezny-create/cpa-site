Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

01 Overview

Sesny Advisory ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data when you use our website and client portal.

02 Information We Collect

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect the following:

  • Personal identification information — your full name, email address, and phone number
  • Financial and tax information — documents you upload through the client portal, including W-2s, 1099s, bank statements, and other tax-related records
  • Account credentials — your email address and encrypted password, managed through Firebase Authentication
  • Usage information — activity within the portal such as document uploads, messages sent, and return status updates
  • Device and browser information — collected automatically by Firebase for security and performance purposes

We do not collect your Social Security Number or Tax ID and store it in our systems. Any such information provided during registration is used solely for account verification and is not retained.

03 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to:

  • Provide tax preparation, bookkeeping, and advisory services
  • Communicate with you about your return or engagement
  • Send you notifications about document requests, status updates, and completed work
  • Maintain accurate records as required by professional and legal standards
  • Improve the security and functionality of the client portal

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, and we do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party under any circumstances.

04 How We Store Your Information

Your data is stored using Firebase, a cloud platform provided by Google LLC. Firebase employs industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and encryption at rest. Your uploaded documents are stored in Firebase Storage and are accessible only to you and to Anthony Sesny.

Our Firestore security rules enforce strict access controls — no other client can access your records, documents, or messages.

05 Who We Share Your Information With

We do not share your personal or financial information with any third party except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Tax authorities — when filing your return with the IRS, state tax agencies, or other required government bodies, as part of the services you have engaged us to provide
  • Legal requirements — if we are required by law, court order, or governmental authority to disclose information
  • With your explicit consent — if you authorize us in writing to share information with a specific third party

06 Data Retention

We retain your tax records and financial documents for a minimum of seven years, consistent with IRS recordkeeping requirements and standard professional practice. After that period, records may be deleted unless you request otherwise.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us directly. Note that records required to be retained by law cannot be deleted prior to the applicable retention period.

07 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sesnyanthony@gmail.com.

08 Cookies and Tracking

Our website does not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes. Firebase may use limited session data to maintain your login state and for security monitoring. No third-party advertising or analytics cookies are used.

09 Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our site, please contact us immediately.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the site following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
